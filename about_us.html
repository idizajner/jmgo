Our Story

From our beginnings as a high-tech company specializes in development and production of smart projector in 2011, we've tried to stay true to our core beliefs—everything you want to watch, to share should be shown on a bigger and bigger screen in a convenient way. We launched JmGO (pronounced like "Jim" plus "Go") , and it has become a revolutionary milestone of our journey. With innovative design and versatile functions, we’ve gained explosive growth and become a formidable supplier of smart projector in the domestic market of China. We are ready to explore the world market, and we hope that you’ll be a part of our awesome journey.
